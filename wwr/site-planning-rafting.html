Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create an easily accesible location for our customers to find our rates, hours, and services we offer, as well as a way to contact us to ask us more specific questions

Audience

The target audience for this website is the family vacation, i.e. tourists. Whether they are coming from far away, or from the nearest town, we want our site to reach those that want to play in the water.
