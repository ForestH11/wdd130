Overview

Purpose

Are you doing the basics of taking care of yourself like maintaining a good diet and exercise, but still feel like your quality of life isn't what you want it to be? If your doctor has signed off on your health and encouraged you to maintain your diet and exercise, but you feel like you aren't getting the results you want, that's where we come in. We can help you formulate a plan for how to eat, what exercises to do, and how you can structure your week to get over that hump and start feeling more like you want to. Generic Wellness helps everyone from teenagers to seniors live a more healthy and happy life.

Audience

People looking to improve their quality of life, locals mostly at first most likely

Content

Home page

Each of us have differing likes and dislikes, strengths and weaknesses, and varying levels of fitness for the activities we perform on a regular basis. Here at Generic Wellness, we want to help you do what you love without having to hold back because you aren't as healthy or fit as you would like to be. From nutrition to exercise, to physical limitations, we can help you do what you love better. For some that may mean a greater level of proficiency, for others it can be as simple as feeling better while going about your day to day. Whatever the case, we are here to help you jump that extra inch, feel better about yourself as you share your artwork, or even get to touching your toes!

Services

Want to meet us? We are open Monday through Friday, 10 to 8. We'd love to see you, and walk-ins are welcome! Been in before? You can request an appointment online! Just click on the “Request Appointment” below, input your contact information, and we'll respond within 24 hours through your medium of choice, be it text, phone call, or email! What services do we offer? We have onsite diet and nutrition experts, as well as personal trainers that can show you exercises to do on our equipment, or if you prefer to work out without equipment, they can show you exercises to do using just your own body weight and enough space to lay down and stand up. Our diet and nutrition experts will work with you to form a meal plan that meets your needs. If you're looking for something more budget friendly, alternatives to your favorite foods, or something you can make in a hurry, each of our food experts will be more than happy to work with you.
